Sold house prices in SW6 (Fulham)

SW6 covers Fulham in the London Borough of Hammersmith and Fulham, from Parsons Green and Fulham Broadway down to Sands End and the river. It is a predominantly Victorian and Edwardian terraced area, with prime pockets such as the Peterborough Estate and Munster Village.
